Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the event?

Please bring a chair or blanket for additional comfort on the grass at Mapleside.

Is there a cost to attend?

The event is free to attend!

What happens if it rains?

Due to the outdoor nature of the event, inclement weather could necessitate a cancellation or reschedule. Be sure to RSVP to get updates if the weather looks questionable.

Is this event suitable for children?

While children are welcome, please note this is primarily a focused, contemplative worship experience. Parents should use their discretion based on their children's ability to participate respectfully.

Will there be food available?

Mapleside facilities will be closed, and there will be no food or services on-site.

How long will the event last?

The evening is expected to run for approximately 90 minutes.
